# Permit Application Summary -- Riverside Development

## Project Description

Clark County Development Corp proposes a mixed-use development on
240 hectares of former agricultural and industrial land along the
Riverside corridor. The project includes 1,200 residential units,
45,000 square meters of commercial space, and 28 hectares of public
open space.

## Regulatory Framework

The project requires approvals under the State Environmental Policy
Act (SEPA), a Shoreline Substantial Development Permit for work
within 60 meters of the ordinary high-water mark, and a Phase II
Municipal Stormwater Permit for post-construction runoff management.

The applicant submitted a complete SEPA checklist on March 15, 2025.
The lead agency issued a Determination of Significance on April 2,
requiring preparation of an Environmental Impact Statement.

## Public Comment Period

The 45-day public comment period runs from May 1 through June 14,
2025. Written comments may be submitted to the Clark County Department
of Community Development at the address listed in the notice of
application.

Three public meetings are scheduled: May 8, May 22, and June 5, all
at the Riverside Community Center from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM.

## Key Review Areas

The scoping process identified four areas requiring detailed analysis:
stormwater management and water quality, traffic and transportation
impacts, habitat and species of concern, and noise during construction.

Each topic area is addressed in a dedicated chapter of the
Environmental Impact Statement, with mitigation measures proposed
where significant adverse impacts are identified.
